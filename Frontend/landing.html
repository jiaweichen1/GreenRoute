How it works

Enter your route and time window. GreenRoute matches drivers and riders heading the same way.

1

Offer or request a ride

Origin, destination, and a flexible time window — that’s it.

2

Get matched automatically

We score nearby routes by proximity and timing to suggest the best fit.

3

Ride together

Confirm the match, chat in-app, and roll. Cheaper, cleaner, easier.
